# Style Recipe: Modern Corporate

- **Visual Mood**: Clean, professional, minimal, crisp, and sophisticated.
- **Pexels Search Direction**: Bright offices, glass architecture, boardroom tables, high-rise views, clean lines.
- **Colour Grade Guidance**: Neutral to slightly cool tint, low opacity, bright and clear contrast.
- **Typography Mood**: Clean, structured, highly legible, medium to bold weight. Pure white text.
- **Best Template Pairing**: `tactical` and `legacy`.
- **Gemini Lighting Direction**: Even, bright corporate lighting, soft shadows, clean and balanced studio illumination.
