# Style Recipe: America's Top Agents

- **Visual Mood**: 'High-Stakes Real Estate'. Bold, modern, fast-moving. Red urgency against clean white power.
- **Pexels Search Direction**: Luxury penthouse living room, modern skyscraper windows, high-rise city view, glass architecture dramatic.
- **Colour Grade Guidance**: Bold red (#CC0000) primary, clean white (#FFFFFF) secondary. High contrast, modern, sharp. Fast-paced energy.
- **Rim Light Colour**: Bright white — crisp, modern, architectural precision.
- **Atmospheric Treatment**: Red diagonal motion swipes or streaks in background layer implying speed and action. City skyline bokeh in background. Clean, no dust or grit — premium modern aesthetic.
- **Gemini Lighting Direction**: Bold red and modern white grading. Background high-end property interior or city penthouse view. Add red diagonal motion swipes in the background layer to imply fast-paced action. Subject should be sharp and confidently lit. Clean directional key light from upper left, white rim light on right edge.
- **Best Template Pairing**: `tactical` and `legacy`.
