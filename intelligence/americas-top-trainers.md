# Style Recipe: America's Top Trainers

- **Visual Mood**: 'Peak Kinetic Energy'. Gritty, raw, explosive. High-frequency intensity, physical power.
- **Pexels Search Direction**: Industrial dark gym, warehouse fitness, crossfit background, dark arena, steel chains dramatic lighting.
- **Colour Grade Guidance**: Industrial gray base, fire orange (#FF4500) accent. High contrast, desaturated mid-tones, punchy shadows. Gritty film grain.
- **Rim Light Colour**: Fire orange — intense, hot, aggressive. Hard edge, not soft.
- **Atmospheric Treatment**: Floating iron-filing particles and fire sparks in foreground. Orange sparks partially overlapping subject shoulders. Heavy film grain texture. Smoke wisps at ground level.
- **Gemini Lighting Direction**: Gritty high-frequency detail. Industrial gray and fire-orange grading. Add floating iron-filing particles and fire sparks in the foreground overlapping the subject's edges. Subject must have high-contrast sweat/muscle definition highlights. Hard dramatic side lighting from upper left, deep shadows.
- **Best Template Pairing**: `tactical`.
