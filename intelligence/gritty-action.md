# Style Recipe: Gritty Action

- **Visual Mood**: High contrast, intense, bold, dramatic, and serious.
- **Pexels Search Direction**: Dark environments, urban night, spotlight, intense atmosphere.
- **Colour Grade Guidance**: Dark vignette, cool or desaturated tint, deep shadows (`#080808`), high opacity.
- **Typography Mood**: Heavy, bold, impactful, uppercase focus.
- **Best Template Pairing**: `tactical`.
- **Gemini Lighting Direction**: Harsh spotlight, dramatic shadows, high contrast, rim lighting to separate subject from dark background.
