# Style Recipe: Kingdom by Creator

- **Visual Mood**: 'Ethereal Influence'. Expansive, divine, sky-blue and radiant gold. Spiritual authority.
- **Pexels Search Direction**: Beautiful mountain sunrise, expansive city skyline from above, vast landscape golden hour, god rays through clouds.
- **Colour Grade Guidance**: Sky blue base, radiant gold (#C9A84C) highlights. Warm top light, luminous. Open, vast, hopeful.
- **Rim Light Colour**: Radiant warm gold — top-down 'God-ray' quality, simulating divine sunlight.
- **Atmospheric Treatment**: Heavy warm lens flare at top-right corner simulating the sun. Soft cloud wisps or light haze in midground. Sun flare bloom across the upper frame.
- **Gemini Lighting Direction**: Top-down 'God-ray' lighting. Sky-blue and radiant gold grading. Background vast blurred landscape. Heavy warm lens flare at top-right corner to simulate the sun. Subject lit from above with gold rim light as if standing in direct sunlight.
- **Best Template Pairing**: `ornate` and `legacy`.
