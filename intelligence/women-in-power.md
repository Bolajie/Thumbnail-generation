# Style Recipe: Women In Power

- **Visual Mood**: Vibrant, high-energy, 'Sovereignty'. Electric, bold, commanding.
- **Pexels Search Direction**: Modern neon abstract architecture, purple and blue lighting, electric city skyline at night.
- **Colour Grade Guidance**: Electric purple (#6A0DAD) primary, magenta accent, deep navy shadows. High saturation, vivid.
- **Rim Light Colour**: Magenta or electric purple — radial neon light framing the subject silhouette.
- **Atmospheric Treatment**: Abstract floating geometric glass shards in midground. Neon light bloom on hair edges (light wrap). Radial neon frames.
- **Gemini Lighting Direction**: Electric purple and magenta colour grading. Surround the subject with radial neon light frames. Apply soft glow to hair edges as light wrap. Background: abstract neon architecture, blurred. Upward dramatic lighting from below and sides.
- **Best Template Pairing**: `tactical` and `ornate`.
