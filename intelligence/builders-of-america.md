# Style Recipe: Builders of America

- **Visual Mood**: 'Foundational Strength'. Rugged, industrial, powerful. Orange and steel in the American grain.
- **Pexels Search Direction**: Construction site steel beams, bridge architecture, sunset skyline cranes, industrial structure golden hour.
- **Colour Grade Guidance**: Construction orange (#FF6B35) primary, slate blue (#4A7B9D) secondary. Heavy film grain and dusty texture. Warm industrial light.
- **Rim Light Colour**: Construction orange — warm, industrial, hard-edged.
- **Atmospheric Treatment**: Heavy film grain and dusty texture throughout. Foreground bokeh of orange safety lights partially overlapping subject. Dust motes in the air. Steel-blue shadow regions.
- **Gemini Lighting Direction**: Construction-orange and steel-blue grading. Apply heavy film-grain and dusty texture. Background blurred steel structures or cranes. Foreground bokeh of orange safety lights partially overlapping the subject. Warm directional light from upper right as if from a setting sun behind steel structures.
- **Best Template Pairing**: `tactical` and `legacy`.
