# Style Recipe: Mompreneurs

- **Visual Mood**: 'Modern Hustle'. Warm, inviting, aspirational. Rose gold meets polished ambition.
- **Pexels Search Direction**: Bright minimalist home office, coffee cup on desk, soft sunlight through window, clean white workspace.
- **Colour Grade Guidance**: Rose gold (#B76E79) primary, warm cream secondary. Soft, high-key, warm. Low drama, high elegance.
- **Rim Light Colour**: Warm rose-gold — soft, flattering, diffuse. No harsh edges.
- **Atmospheric Treatment**: Warm bokeh light orbs in foreground to create soft 3D sandwich effect. Floating light orbs partially overlapping subject shoulders. Soft window-light flare.
- **Gemini Lighting Direction**: Warm rose-gold and cream tones. Soft and inviting lighting. Background blurred home office. Add warm bokeh light orbs to the foreground, partially overlapping the subject's shoulders to create a soft 3D depth effect. Soft diffuse lighting from large window on subject's right.
- **Best Template Pairing**: `ornate` and `legacy`.
