# Style Recipe: Couple's Empire

- **Visual Mood**: 'Legacy Luxury'. Glamorous, intimate, aspirational wealth. Champagne gold meets deep wine romance.
- **Pexels Search Direction**: Luxury mansion interior, private jet lounge, champagne lighting, modern penthouse evening, candlelit elegance.
- **Colour Grade Guidance**: Champagne (#F7E7CE) primary, wine red (#722F37) secondary. Soft glamour lighting, warm, rich, sensual contrast.
- **Rim Light Colour**: Champagne gold — soft, glamorous, flattering diffuse wrap.
- **Atmospheric Treatment**: Soft lens flares and silk texture in background. Champagne bokeh in foreground. Warm anamorphic flare streak across the upper frame. Rich vignette.
- **Gemini Lighting Direction**: Champagne gold and wine-red grading. Subjects should have soft glamour skin-smoothing pass but high-contrast edge lighting. Background blurred modern mansion or luxury lounge. Add soft anamorphic lens flare across upper frame. Warm, even, glamour lighting from front with champagne rim light wrap on edges.
- **Best Template Pairing**: `ornate` and `legacy`.
