# Style Recipe: America's Best Doctors

- **Visual Mood**: 'Clinical Precision'. High-key, clean, bright. Sterile authority, modern medicine.
- **Pexels Search Direction**: Modern medical laboratory, blurred hospital hallway, high-tech clinic, clean white surgical theater.
- **Colour Grade Guidance**: Cyan (#00FFFF) highlights, sterile white base, very high brightness. Near-monochrome with cyan accent.
- **Rim Light Colour**: Soft clean white — bright, clinical, precise. No warmth.
- **Atmospheric Treatment**: Faint ECG pulse-line graphic behind the subject. Clean bokeh from clinic lights in background. Minimal atmospheric debris — cleanliness is the aesthetic.
- **Gemini Lighting Direction**: High-key bright lighting. Cyan and sterile white grading. Faint ECG pulse-line graphic placed behind the subject. Subject should have soft clean white rim light. Extremely sharp and clean — no grit, no grain. Even soft-box style overhead lighting.
- **Best Template Pairing**: `tactical` and `legacy`.
