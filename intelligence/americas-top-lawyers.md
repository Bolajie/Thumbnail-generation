# Style Recipe: America's Top Lawyers

- **Visual Mood**: 'Gavel & Grace'. Authoritative, prestigious, warm mahogany gravitas.
- **Pexels Search Direction**: Classic law library, supreme court interior, wood grain columns, courthouse marble, sunbeams through tall windows.
- **Colour Grade Guidance**: Warm mahogany (#4A0E0E) base, law gold accent, deep brown shadows. Warm, rich, high contrast.
- **Rim Light Colour**: Law gold / warm amber — matching the sunbeam shafts in the background.
- **Atmospheric Treatment**: Heavy 'mote' dust particles floating in visible light paths. God rays through tall windows. Warm vignette.
- **Gemini Lighting Direction**: Warm mahogany and gold tones. Background must be a blurred courtroom or mahogany office with sunbeams visible. Add heavy mote dust particles floating in the light paths to create depth. Directional warm light from upper right as if through tall court windows.
- **Best Template Pairing**: `legacy` and `ornate`.
