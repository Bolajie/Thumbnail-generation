# Style Recipe: Legacy Makers

- **Visual Mood**: Prestigious, timeless, 'Hall of Fame' gravitas. Rich blacks, dramatic gold.
- **Pexels Search Direction**: Dark luxury library interior, leather books, spotlighting, oak shelves, golden ambient light.
- **Colour Grade Guidance**: Deep black base (#080808), warm gold tint (#C9A84C) at moderate opacity, high contrast.
- **Rim Light Colour**: #C9A84C — sharp, defined gold rim light on the subject silhouette.
- **Atmospheric Treatment**: Subtle gold dust particles in the midground. Heavy vignette to deepen shadows at edges.
- **Gemini Lighting Direction**: High-contrast black and white grading on the subject. Sharp gold rim light. Background deeply shadowed and blurred historical library. Dramatic side lighting from upper left.
- **Best Template Pairing**: `legacy` and `ornate`.
