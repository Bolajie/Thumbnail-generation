# Style Recipe: Cinematic Gold

- **Visual Mood**: Rich, dramatic, classic, premium, and elegant.
- **Pexels Search Direction**: Search for golden hour lighting, luxurious interiors, deep warm tones, and high-end environments.
- **Colour Grade Guidance**: Warm golden tint (`#C9A84C`), moderate opacity, high contrast with deep blacks (`#080808`).
- **Typography Mood**: Large, bold, authoritative, but elegant. Use rich gold or pure white.
- **Best Template Pairing**: `ornate` and `legacy`.
- **Gemini Lighting Direction**: Dramatic edge lighting, warm golden rim light on the subject, deep shadows, cinematic studio lighting.
