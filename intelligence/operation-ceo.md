# Style Recipe: Operation CEO

- **Visual Mood**: 'Global Command Center'. Strategic, authoritative, high-tech. Teal and orange cinematic tension.
- **Pexels Search Direction**: Cyber security command center, world map monitors, blue tech, war room screens, data center blue neon.
- **Colour Grade Guidance**: Deep navy (#101D42) base, teal highlights, orange accent. High contrast, desaturated background.
- **Rim Light Colour**: Teal/cyan — cold, precise rim light. Orange accent on opposite edge.
- **Atmospheric Treatment**: Subtle digital HUD overlay on background. Blue lens flare across the bottom third. Blueprint or grid texture in background layer.
- **Gemini Lighting Direction**: Teal and orange cinematic grade. Overlay subtle digital HUD onto the background. Subject sharp and center-aligned. Add blue lens flare across the bottom third. Cold top-down lighting with warm orange counter-fill.
- **Best Template Pairing**: `tactical`.
